## Step-by-Step Upload Instructions

### Method 1: Web Browser (Easiest)

1. **Open Google Drive**
   - Go to https://drive.google.com
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Upload the File**
   - Click the "New" button (top left)
   - Select "File upload"
   - Navigate to: `/Users/tarang/CascadeProjects/windsurf-project/shopee-analytics-platform/`
   - Select: `shopee-analytics-submission.zip`
   - Click "Open"

3. **Wait for Upload**
   - You'll see a progress bar
   - Should take less than 10 seconds (96 KB is small)

4. **Get Shareable Link**
   - Right-click on the uploaded file
   - Click "Get link" or "Share"
   - Change access to "Anyone with the link can view"
   - Click "Copy link"
   - Share this link with your instructor

---

### Method 2: Drag and Drop

1. Open Google Drive in your browser
2. Open Finder and navigate to the project folder
3. Drag `shopee-analytics-submission.zip` into Google Drive
4. Wait for upload to complete
5. Get shareable link (see Method 1, step 4)

---

### Method 3: Google Drive Desktop App

If you have Google Drive installed:

1. Open Finder
2. Go to your Google Drive folder
3. Copy `shopee-analytics-submission.zip` into it
4. Wait for sync
5. Open Google Drive in browser to get link
